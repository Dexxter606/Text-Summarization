Text Summarization

Text summarization has become one of the most important yet challenging tasks in Natural Language Processing (NLP). The resurgence of deep learning has greatly advanced this field by neural generation models. Our project focuses on generating News Headlines when provided with a news article. The aim of this is to condense a document while the condensed content retains the core meaning of the original document. The text summarization method consists of extractive summarization and abstractive summarization. Headline generation is an abstractive summarization subtask. To generate headlines that compress the information in a longer text or a shorter text, we need to train a large news dataset either by building a model from scratch or using a pre-trained model.
